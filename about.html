I love imagining new ideas and bringing them to life. I want to make a difference in the world through innovation and invention. I am enjoy thinking through logic-based challenges and coming up with automated solutions. I can learn new skills quickly, am a self-starter, and most importantly I want to learn.

As a child I would play with Legos, and take apart electrical things so I could see how they worked. As a young adult, I would keep busy programming video games,making movies, writing books, and much more. I played trombone for three years, and participated in four musicals and two plays. I attended JEMS (Junior Engineering Math and Science) in high school, which is where my real love for Mechanical Engineering began. I bought the student edition of Solid Works and would create 3D models of things around the house.

I am married to the amazing Devin Mayberry. We are active members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints (Mormons). I enjoy scuba diving, hiking, and creating things.

We have a beautiful baby girl named Lydia. My favorite part of the day is the time I get to spend with her before bedtime.
